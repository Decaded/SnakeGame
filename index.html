    How to Play

    • Use arrow keys (or WSAD) to move (or touch controls on mobile).
    • Avoid the walls and your own tail.
    • Eat food to grow and score points!
    • Each level multiplies point gain and game speed.

    Food Legend

    • Apple - 1 point
    • Cherry - Starts 2x combo
    • Golden - Double your score!
    • Hubris Berry - Risk/reward multiplier
